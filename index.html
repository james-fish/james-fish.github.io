Hi! I'm James.

I’m a design strategist and coach with over a decade of experience in design, UX, and innovation.

I’m passionate about working on problems that affect real people in order to improve the world we live in. Personally compelling to me are healthcare, diversity and inclusion, economic justice, and civic spaces for the arts. Design Strategy

As a design strategist, I turn observations into insights, and insights into testable prototypes, using human-centered research methods.

I work hand-in-hand with organizations to understand people and their needs, then test solutions for those needs in conjunction with the organization’s mission and vision. This might mean creating something new (like a website, product, service, or campaign) or improving on an existing offering.

For more on what design strategy is and can do for you, click here or here.


Offerings:

  • Research & test planning
  • Needfinding & ethnography
  • Value proposition testing
  • Concept testing
  • Roadmap & development strategy
  • User experience & usability testing
Good for:
  • Founders who need to validate an idea — or figure out which problem to solve
  • Early stage companies who need to iron out design issues before launch
  • Established organizations who need to define their core value propositions
  • Product/service teams that need to figure out where to focus their attention

Workshops

About Me

I coach regularly at the HPI d.School and HPI Academy in Potsdam, Germany, guiding students and professionals on real design thinking projects ranging from 3 days to 3 months.

I am also a frequent collaborator with Co.Lab, a decentralized, transnational tech worker cooperative that primarily serves mission-driven organizations through agile web development and cooperative co-design.

I worked for 4 years as a UX researcher at Proteus, a digital health company in Silicon Valley. Previously, I studied sociology and economics at Harvard and learned design thinking at the HPI d.school.

I've lived on three continents and speak 3+ languages (English, German, Italian, and some Turkish.) I’m also deeply involved in the arts as a musician and DJ as well as an interactive event producer. I am currently based in Berlin and visit San Francisco frequently.
