Hi! I'm James.

I’m a design strategist and coach with over a decade of experience in design, UX, and innovation.

I’m passionate about working on problems that affect real people in order to improve the world we live in. Personally compelling to me are issues of diversity and inclusion, economic justice, and civic spaces for the arts. Let’s think and act for the long term — rather than making one investor happy today, why not think about making the world better for us all, tomorrow?

Design Strategy

As a design strategist, I turn observations into insights, and insights into testable prototypes, using human-centered research methods. I work hand-in-hand with organizations to understand people and their needs, then test solutions for those needs in conjunction with the organization’s mission and vision. This might mean creating something new (like a website, product, service, or campaign) or improving on an existing offering. For more on what design strategy is and can do for you, click here.

  • Research & test planning
  • Needfinding & ethnography
  • Value proposition testing
  • Concept testing
  • Roadmap & development strategy
  • User experience & usability testing

Workshops

As an experienced facilitator, I help teams boost creativity, build empathy, and encourage collaborative interactions, drawing from methods in design thinking, mindfulness, and nonviolent communication. I customize each workshop with the client according to their needs - no two workshops are exactly alike.

  • Intro to Design Thinking (1-2 days)
  • Design Sprint (3-5 days)
  • Skills Workshop (1 day)

Good for:

  • Teams who need to establish better collaboration dynamics and an innovation mindset
  • Established organizations that need to advance an idea, product, or service - in just one week

Consulting

Every organization needs the right mix of people, skills, and tools in order to innovate. I help teams assess gaps in their internal design capacity and identify where they most need to focus their attention.

Good for:

  • Investors or incubators seeking an in-house strategist to advise their orgs
  • Founders (or leadership teams) who need a roadmap for their organizational strategy

About Me

I coach regularly at the HPI d.School and HPI Academy in Potsdam, guiding students and professionals on real design thinking projects ranging from 3 days to 3 months.

I am also a frequent collaborator with Co.Lab, a decentralized, transnational tech worker cooperative that primarily serves mission-driven organizations through agile web development and cooperative co-design.

I worked for 4 years as a UX researcher at Proteus, a digital health company in Silicon Valley. Previously, I studied sociology and economics at Harvard and learned design thinking at the HPI d.school.

I've lived on three continents and speak 3+ languages (English, German, Italian, and some Turkish.) I’m also deeply involved in the arts as a musician, DJ, and interactive event producer. I am currently based in Berlin and visit San Francisco frequently.
